關於我

本實驗室致力於將資料探勘、機器學習、生物資訊等資料科學相關技術，應用於疾病預測、臨床決策支援、個人化醫療等醫療相關議題。本研究室著重生物統計學、醫學影像分析、基因組學等專業領域知識的訓練,目標為使研究室成員能結合理論與實務,發展及應用合適的資料科學技術,為醫療健康領域帶來創新突破。

Our laboratory is dedicated to applying data mining, machine learning, bioinformatics and other data science-related techniques to medical issues such as disease prediction, clinical decision support, and personalized medicine. The laboratory emphasizes training in biostatistics, medical image analysis, genomics, and other specialized domains. The goal is to enable lab members to combine theory and practice, developing and applying suitable data science techniques to drive innovative breakthroughs in the medical and healthcare fields.
